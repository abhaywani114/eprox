Advantages of
Professionally Installed Epoxy Floors

While kits, DIY products, and handy-man installations might claim to offer the same benefits as professional-grade garage floor epoxy systems, water-based products and single coats of paint or formula do not share these critical benefits of having your floor installed by professionals.

Extreme floor durability which is resistant to pressure, cracking, stains, chemicals, and abrasions

Endless design possibilities including inlays, patterns, colors, logos, and textures

Protection from moisture, road salt, petrochemicals, and oil, with easy soap and water cleanup

Environmentally friendly—the production of epoxy does not threaten the environment;

Resurfacing damaged, cracked, or stained concrete with a self-leveling smooth finish

Noise-dampening qualities, light-enhancing finishes, and slip-resistant surfaces improve safety
